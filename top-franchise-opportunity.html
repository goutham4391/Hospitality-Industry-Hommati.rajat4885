3D VIRTUAL TOURS

3D VIRTUAL TOURS


Utilizing this technology means that potential buyers can tour a home from anywhere, at their own pace, 24 hours a day, seven days a week.

Technology has completely transformed the online home shopping experience with the introduction of 3D virtual tours. Potential buyers can now tour a home from anywhere at any time before they ever step inside. They can move throughout the space as they wish and turn to look in any direction. This includes looking up to see light fixtures on the ceiling and looking down to see flooring. They can even walk up to the windows of the home to see the view outside. These immersive tours can be taken on a computer, iPad, tablet or smart phone with access to the internet. This experience is far beyond simply looking at photos.

Statistics from real estate websites show that listings with virtual tours get clicked on 40% more than listings without virtual tours.– RIS Media

49% More qualified leads come from 3D virtual tours.– RIS Media

AERIAL DRONE VIDEOS


Capture the attention of home buyers with aerial drone video and photography. Drones offer versatile and creative ways to capture a property and showcase unique details of a home and the surrounding property especially for those properties where traditional photos are not adequate. Home buyers rely on visual marketing to make their buying decisions. The more visuals a listing has, the more engaged and interested potential buyers will be. Having these digital assets also makes a listing stand out from all of the others and can make a great impression with potential home buyers.
Homes listed with video get four times the inquiries of homes listed without video. – INMAN®
Today’s home sellers want to work with real estate agents that use aerial drone photography and videos to market their homes. The National Association of REALTORS® (NAR) indicates that using drones for real estate is quickly becoming the industry’s new normal and innovative companies are taking advantage of this to attract clientele.

High-quality listing photos help real estate agents improve credibility, strengthen their brand and grow their clientele. Buyers love FLOOR PLANS

Include floor plans in online listings to appeal to buyers.

CURB LEADS


This is one of the highest quality leads we can deliver to agents and it is not dependent on website traffic. These leads are self-generated from real potential buyers who are physically outside a home looking at it. When a buyer texts the code from the sign rider to get the listing information, the agent immediately receives that buyer’s phone number so the agent will never lose another drive-by lead.
42% of all calls made by potential home buyers from for sale signs go to voicemail, and most drive-bys don’t leave messages. – INMAN®
15 years ago, 16% of leads generated for a Real Estate Agent were from their “For Sale” signs. Today, “For Sale” signs only account for 7% of an agent’s total leads. Agents are losing more than 50% of their sign leads.

GET LEADS IN REAL TIME

Here’s how you easily can double your drive-by inquiries.
  • 1. Start a Free 30-Day Trial and add your Sign Riders to your listings. Sign riders are customized for you with your own code, phone number and firm colors.
  • 2. Buyers text the code shown on the sign rider to the local SMS number located on your sign rider.
  • 3. Buyers receive a link with your listing on their phone.
  • 4. Buyers open the link on their phone to see the complete listing that includes the price, photos and information.
  • 5. In real-time, you instantly receive a text back with the buyer’s phone number and the listing they inquired about. So, you’ll be able to reach them while they’re still at the listing.

Mr. Clum will tell you the exciting story of why he created Hommati, why it is a franchise you should consider, and some of the benefits of being a franchise owner. If you have a spouse or partner, regardless whether they are going to be involved in the business or not, it would be beneficial to have them on this call.

After the conference call you will be sent an email with a link to the Hommati Franchise Application to fill out then take the EQ Test – Entrepreneurial Quotient Test and a personality profile questionnaire for business ownership. We will also do a background check consisting of criminal history, driving record and credit check. This is a soft inquiry, it will not affect your credit score. If you have some minor things in your background that you may be worried about, please understand we look at this as one piece of your overall qualifications. The only areas that would automatically disqualify you are a felony conviction, a major driving infraction, bankruptcy in the last seven years or, unless there is a unique situation, a FICO score under 650.

He will then do a deep dive into our business model and explain our Unique Selling Propositions, our marketing strategies, royalties and territory. If both you and Mr. Clum feels it is mutually beneficial to continue to the next step. If you have a spouse or partner, regardless whether they are going to be involved in the business or not, it would be beneficial to have them on this call.

Once you return from training, you will complete any remaining start-up items on your start-up checklist.

You are then ready to start operating your Hommati franchise.
